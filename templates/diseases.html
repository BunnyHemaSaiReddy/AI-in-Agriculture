Powdery Mildew

A fungal disease causing white powdery spots on leaves and stems.

Downy Mildew

Appears as yellow patches on leaves with gray fuzzy growth underneath.

Mosaic Virus

Causes mottled, yellowed leaves with stunted growth.

Leaf Spot

Small, dark spots on leaves that may enlarge over time.

Anthracnose

Causes dark, sunken lesions on leaves, stems, and fruits.

Rust

Orange or reddish pustules appear on leaf surfaces.

Bacterial Spot

Small, dark water-soaked lesions on leaves and fruits.

Early Blight

Brown spots with concentric rings on older leaves.

Late Blight

Causes large, dark lesions and spreads rapidly.

Yellow Mosaic

Yellow patches and curling leaves caused by viral infection.

Leaf Curl

Leaves curl, turn yellow, and show stunted growth.

Aphids

Small insects that feed on plant sap, causing distortion.
